About NewsNest

NewsNest is a personalized news aggregator built for absolutely everyone — not just professionals or journalists. Whether you're preparing for the UPSC exam, tracking the stock market, following your favorite cricket team, or just curious about the latest in AI, NewsNest curates a homepage tailored to what matters to you.

We aggregate articles from a trusted news source and organize them across 20+ interest categories — from Government Policies and International Affairs to Startups, Health, Sports, Science, and Entertainment. You choose your interests once, and your homepage adapts automatically.

🔒

Privacy First

Your name, interests and bookmarks stay in your browser — never on a server.

🌐

One Trusted Source

We aggregate from a single reliable news API, normalized for consistency.

⚙️

Modern Architecture

Built with a microservices architecture, containerized with Docker and Kubernetes-ready.
